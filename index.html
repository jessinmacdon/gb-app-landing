FAQs

Questions, answered.

Find answers to common questions about GlobalBuy24 and our services.

How does GlobalBuy24 work?

Copy the product link, paste it in our app, and receive a quote. After payment, we purchase the item, handle shipping and customs, and deliver it to a local pickup point near you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept local Mobile Money options including MTN and Orange, plus standard bank cards — secure, instant, and confirmed in the app.

Are there hidden fees?

No. Your quote includes the product price, international shipping and our service fee. The total you approve is the total you pay — nothing is added at pickup.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders arrive within 10–21 days, with 14 days as our current average. You can follow every stage — purchased, shipped, in customs, ready — inside the app.

Where can I pick up my order?

Our pickup network covers Douala and Yaoundé today, with new points opening regularly. You choose your preferred location at checkout and we notify you the moment your package arrives.
