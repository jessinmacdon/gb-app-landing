Find answers to common questions about GlobalBuy24 and our services.

How does it work?

GlobalBuy24 simplifies international shopping. Just copy the product link, paste it in our app, and receive a quote. After payment, your order will be delivered to a local pickup point.

What payment methods?

We accept various local payment methods, including Mobile Money options like MTN and Orange. This ensures a seamless and secure transaction experience.

Are there hidden fees?

No, we provide a full quote upfront with no hidden charges. Transparency is key to our service. You can shop with confidence knowing the total cost before you pay.
