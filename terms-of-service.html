6. How GB24 Works

  1. Placing an Order: Users provide product links from third-party online stores. GB24 processes the request and provides a comprehensive quote including the product price, our service fee, and the estimated shipping cost to the final destination.
  2. Payment: Once the quote is confirmed and accepted by the user, full payment must be made before GB24 places the order with the third-party retailer. Payments are processed via secure third-party payment providers as detailed in Section 7.
  3. Shipping to GB24 Hub: The items are purchased and delivered from the original online store to our designated Berlin hub in Germany. Users are responsible for ensuring the retailer can ship to this address.
  4. Processing & Shipping: Once received at our Berlin hub, we conduct an external package condition check, consolidate shipments (if multiple items are ordered), and then arrange for onward shipping to the final destination in selected African countries.
  5. Delivery: Users collect their orders at pre-determined designated pick-up points or via local couriers where applicable in the destination country. Specific delivery instructions and pick-up point details will be communicated to the user.
