  • The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (Article 77 GDPR): You have the right to complain to a data protection authority about Our collection and use of Your Personal Data. For more information, please contact Your local data protection authority in the EEA or the UK. In Germany, this would be the Berlin Commissioner for Data Protection and Freedom of Information (Berliner Beauftragte für Datenschutz und Informationsfreiheit).
